# Business Model: Independent Forestry Operations

## Classification

- Repository: `cloud-itonami-isco-6210`
- ISCO-08: `6210`
- Occupation: Forestry and Related Workers
- Social impact: rural-livelihoods, worker-safety, environment

## Customer

- woodlot owners
- timber cooperatives
- conservation land managers

## Offer

- stand surveying
- harvest planning
- felling-support scheduling
- replanting tracking
- compliance reporting

## Revenue

- per-harvest fee
- monthly operations platform fee
- reporting package

## Trust Controls

- no felling-support dispatch without governor gate
- protected-habitat boundaries cannot be silently overridden
- harvest records are auditable, not editable
